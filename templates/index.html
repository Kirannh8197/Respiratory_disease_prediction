Predict respiratory diseases using audio files.
A Respiratory Disease Prediction System is a medical diagnostic tool designed to analyze respiratory sounds (like breathing, wheezing, and crackles) and predict conditions such as Asthma, Pneumonia, and Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease (COPD). This system leverages machine learning and signal processing techniques to assist healthcare providers in early diagnosis and management of respiratory diseases.
